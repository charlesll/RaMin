# Datasets

## Training dataset

Training files are in excellent_unoriented, those are spectra from the RRUFF database and available online

Two obs.npy and labels.pkl files contain the X spectra and their labels, respectively.

## Files to further test the method

Those files are in the To_Recognize folder, they are from the IPGP Geomaterial Raman database.

Again, two obs.npy and labels.pkl files contain the X spectra and their labels, respectively.
